About Us

Kenya Conservatory is a platform that hosts details of the “Big Five” animals in the country’s Game Parks and natural reserves. The web applications allows visitors to learn unique facts about the animals with the provision being directed to resources and repositories for further details.

Kenya Conservatory is an open, free and easy-to-access platform whose purpose is to display Kenya’s wealthy wildlife. The Big Five account for 70% of Kenya’s wildlife tourist attraction index, implying that the animals are beneficial to the economy. The platform is interactive, allowing our visitors to give feedback on their experience and suggestions on how we can make it resourceful in our iteration.

Kenya conservatory is a website that seeks to teach the world about our local wildlife within the current borders of Eastern Africa,
and to further teach future generations about our wildlife giving them a sound safe platform from which information can be attained in a manner suitable for the present and future.

In recent years we have seen the decline of wildlife due to poaching and other malicious activites, and while some of the activities like farming
may not be considered harmful, going unchecked as an economic activity has been seen to be highly detrimental.
